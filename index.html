Empowering youth to get Along and get Ahead


To promote economic self-sufficiency through peace and employability for opportunity youth.


To equip youth ages 13-25 years old with essential tools to lead peaceful relationships and succeed in today's workforce. WDGA teaches students how to avert violence, become civic leaders and attract employment opportunities to promote community and economic development.


Bringing Hope to the Youth

What we do

The Workforce Development Global Alliance (WDGA) promotes economic self-sufficiency through employment opportunities for tomorrow’s workforce, our youth. Global corporate alliances and awareness provide for education, exposure, empowerment, experience and employment opportunities. All programs engage the youth in tying it all together with our governing values of Teamwork, Integrity and Excellence! All resulting in improved academic performance and quality of life!
